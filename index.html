Chemical Biology of Nucleic

We are a group of motivated young researchers supervised by Professor Masad Damha. At McGill since 1992, research in our lab takes place at the interface of organic synthesis and chemical biology. We are involved in studying the effects of chemical modification on the biological activity and structure of nucleic acids.

Other interests in our lab focus on a range of topics relevant to nucleic acid chemistry, including branched RNAs, DNA secondary structures, strategies to improve cellular uptake of nucleic acids, and the design and synthesis of novel nucleoside analogs.
